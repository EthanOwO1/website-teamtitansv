WHO WE ARE

We are Team 27316, a team comprised of six first-year members and we proudly represent St. Augustine CHS in the First Tech Challenge.

Our team’s goals for this season is to: learn as much as possible, create a reliable and consistent robot, document our work in a organized way, make it to the provincial championships and finally, engage our school/community.
