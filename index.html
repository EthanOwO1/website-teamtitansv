WHO ARE THE TITANS?

Team Titans V is more than just a robotics club; we are a community of innovators, problem solvers, and future engineers. Founded in 2019, our mission is to push the boundaries of high school robotics while fostering leadership and technical skills.

We design, build, and program competitive robots for state and national championships. From complex mechanical lifts to autonomous vision programming, every bolt and line of code represents our passion.
